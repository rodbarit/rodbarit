Professional Profile

A Career at the
Intersection of Law,
Justice & Innovation

Dr. Irene D. Valones is a distinguished Court Attorney of the Supreme Court of the Philippines with a rare breadth of academic distinction and international recognition. She is a subject matter expert in Forensics, Cybercrime, and Cybersecurity — fields where law meets the frontier of technology.

A product of the Philippines' most prestigious institutions and an alumna of King's College London through the UK Chevening Scholarship, Dr. Valones brings a truly global perspective to her practice. Her work spans judicial service, law professorship, international arbitration, and forensic science — representing the gold standard of cross-disciplinary legal expertise.

"Advancing digital justice through transnational governance of cyber forensics and evidence acquisition in the post-pandemic era."

She is a Professor of International Law and Constitutional Law at Arellano University School of Law, Tomas Claudio College of Law, and the Graduate School of Law at UST — where she also serves as Associate Professor and Editor-in-Chief of the UST Law Journal.
