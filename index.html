About

A quantitative researcher and finance professional with over a decade of demonstrated excellence across investment management, portfolio management, and systematic trading research.

Currently serving as a Quantitative Researcher at Phitopolis and Lecturer at Ateneo de Manila University, Rod designs and refines systematic investment strategies by bridging rigorous academic research with practical application. He leverages Python, machine learning, and advanced statistical modeling to develop predictive trading signals and optimize portfolio performance.

Rod's career spans institutional investment management at Sun Life and Bank of the Philippine Islands, where he managed equity portfolios across retirement and pre-need accounts. As a UK Government Chevening Scholar, he pursued an MSc in Finance Analytics at King's College London — publishing research on empirical asset pricing via machine learning in emerging markets.

Beyond research, Rod is a Toastmasters charter president, civic technology advocate, and community builder — connecting professionals at the intersection of finance, technology, and public service.

Core Advocacy

Financial inclusion through technology

Leveraging quantitative finance, machine learning, and fintech to make financial systems more accessible, equitable, and efficient — particularly for underserved communities in emerging markets.
